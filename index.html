LuxBrands Success Story

Challenge

LuxBrands, a premium fashion and accessories company, was losing millions annually to counterfeit products. Their traditional authentication methods were easily replicated by sophisticated counterfeiters, damaging brand reputation and customer trust.

Solution

Lochness Group developed a blockchain-based authentication system using Chia's secure and sustainable blockchain:

  • Created unique digital certificates for each product with cryptographic proof of authenticity
  • Embedded NFC tags in products linked to blockchain certificates for seamless verification
  • Developed a consumer mobile app for instant authentication and ownership registration
  • Implemented a secure ownership transfer protocol for secondary market sales
  • Built a real-time analytics dashboard to track verification patterns and detect potential fraud

Results

  • 80% reduction in counterfeit products within key markets

  • Created new direct marketing channel through authentication app with 68% engagement rate

  2. Accept Offer: Use your Chia wallet to accept the offer, which creates an escrow smart contract.
  3. Work Begins: Once the contract is established, your specialist will contact you to begin work.
  4. Milestone Payments: Funds are released from escrow as work milestones are completed and verified.
  5. Contract Completion: Upon successful completion, final payment is released and the contract is closed.
